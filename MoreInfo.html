About The Game

Trig Wars puts a spin on the original asteroids video game in a way that makes it educational. It is a video game designed to help others get a good understanding of degrees, radians, and their relations to the trigonometric functions. It was designed using Unity Software a leading game engine currently being used in major video games and simulations. In addition to the use of Unity software, the scripts were written in C#, an object-oriented programming language. Once completed, the game was then embedded into this website which was written using HTML and CSS.

Gameplay

The objective is to destroy all asteroids using the limited ammunition before the asteroids destroy your spaceship. Once a category (Degrees, Radians, or Sin and Cos) and difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard, or Endless) are selected, the player will be placed into the game. In the top right, you will see the menu button for if you would like to exit the game. In the top left, you will see a counter for the players: Score, Ammunition, and Remaining Asteroids. In order to aim and fire the missiles in a specific direction, you must click on the related button found on one of the two panels found on either side of the screen.

It must be noted that within the Trigonomentric category (Sin and Cos), it is equivalent to the unit circle typically learned in Pre-Calculus. Therefore, the first term is equivalent to the cosine of the angle and the second term is equivalent to the sine of the angle.
